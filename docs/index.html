Match rating (doubles):
mr = 0.61 × (opp1 + opp2 − partner) + 0.39 × your_dynamic_rating + 0.74 × gd_pct

Match rating (singles):
mr = 0.61 × opp + 0.39 × your_dynamic_rating + 0.13 × gd_pct

where gd_pct = (games_won − games_lost) / total_games

Dynamic rating (after each match):
dynamic = (mr_now + prev_rating_1 + prev_rating_2 + prev_rating_3) ÷ 4
Your new dynamic rating is the average of this match's rating and your 3 most recent prior dynamic ratings. Load your TennisRecord link (or enter manually) to seed those 3 prior values accurately.
